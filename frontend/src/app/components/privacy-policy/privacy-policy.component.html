NO TRACKERS, NO COOKIES, NO JAVASCRIPT?

Out of respect for the Bit community, this website does not use any third-party analytics, third-party advertising, or third-party trackers of any kind.

We also do not make any external calls to any third-party service, except for optionally loading fonts from Google Fonts for better readability and language support. This can be disabled in settings.

This website is a single-page application (SPA) that requires JavaScript to function. We understand some users prefer to disable JavaScript for privacy, but this website is a data visualization tool that needs JavaScript to dynamically load and display blockchain data.

Out of respect for the Bit community, this website does not use any first-party cookies, except to store your preferred language and other display settings. These settings are stored locally in your browser and are not sent to our servers.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you access our Website, we may automatically collect standard server log information, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and referring website addresses. This information is used for system administration, troubleshooting, and to ensure the security and integrity of our Website. Log files are deleted on a regular basis (typically after 7 days).

If you use the search function on our Website, we may temporarily log your search queries to improve our search functionality. These search logs are not tied to your IP address and are regularly deleted.

TRUST YOUR OWN SELF-HOSTED B1tpool EXPLORER

For maximum privacy, we recommend that you use your own self-hosted instance of the B1tpool explorer on your own full node, such as with Umbrel, RaspiBlitz, myNode, etc.

Running your own instance of B1tpool ensures that your IP address and search queries are not shared with any third party, including us. Many Bit fullnode distributions offer a one-click installation method. See our GitHub repository for more information on self-hosting.
